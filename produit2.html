Galaxy A53 5G

In addition to its lightweight and compact design, the Samsung Galaxy A53 5G features a screen with a resolution of 2,400 x 1,080 pixels. It is made with Gorilla Glass 5, providing impressive durability. The phone offers a battery capacity of 5,000 mAh, compared to just 4,500 mAh for its predecessor.
